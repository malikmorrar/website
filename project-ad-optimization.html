Overview

The Problem

Managing Google Ads campaigns across multiple clients manually is slow, error-prone, and doesn't scale. Campaign managers spend hours pulling data, spotting trends, and adjusting bids — time that could be better spent on strategy. Performance gaps go unnoticed until they've already cost money.

The Solution

I built an AI-assisted analytics pipeline that automatically ingests Google Ads performance data, tracks key KPIs, and uses AI-driven analysis to surface optimization opportunities. The system automatically adjusts bids based on performance insights and generates real-time dashboards for full campaign visibility — turning what was a manual daily process into an automated, always-on system.

Pipeline
01
Data Ingestion
Automatically pulls live performance data from the Google Ads API — impressions, clicks, CTR, CPC, conversions, and cost per lead across all client accounts
02
AI Performance Analysis
AI evaluates CTR, CPC, and conversion trends to identify underperforming keywords, ad groups, and campaigns — surfacing actionable optimization opportunities
03
Automated Bid Adjustment
Based on AI insights, the system automatically adjusts keyword bids and targeting parameters to improve efficiency — reducing manual intervention by ~30%
04
Real-Time Dashboards
Live visualizations surface campaign performance, trend data, and optimization impact — enabling fast, data-driven decision making
